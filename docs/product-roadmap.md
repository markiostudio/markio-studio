# Markio Product Roadmap

High-level delivery phases for Markio. Scope can shift, but each phase builds on Business Brain as the source of truth.

## Phase 1

Foundation: understand the business and ship the first usable outputs.

- **Business Brain** — extract, confirm, and persist the business intelligence object
- **Landing Page** — generate a full landing page preview from the brain
- **Business Report** — launch report with summary, health scores, and AI recommendations

## Phase 2

Multi-business scale, persistence, and publishable web.

- **Projects** — multiple businesses, each with its own brain and assets
- **Supabase** — authenticated accounts and cloud persistence (beyond localStorage)
- **Website Generator** — multi-page website generation from Business Brain
- **AI Images** — generate hero/about/gallery imagery from brain image prompts
- **Publish** — go live with domains / hosted previews

## Phase 3

Full marketing stack and growth tooling.

- **Brand Kit** — logo, palette, fonts, and brand voice pack
- **Ads** — paid ad creatives and variants
- **Social** — social post calendars and copy
- **Emails** — nurture / campaign sequences
- **CRM** — leads, clients, and follow-up workflows
- **Analytics** — performance visibility across assets and channels
