# Markio Branding

## Product name

**Markio**

## Tagline

**Your AI Marketing Employee**

## Mission

Help every business owner market like a professional without hiring an agency.

## Target audience

Primary local / SMB verticals:

- Restaurants
- Beauty
- Dental
- Real Estate
- Gym
- SMEs (small and medium businesses more broadly)

## Tone

- **Premium** — polished, high-trust, agency-quality output
- **Modern** — clean product feel, current design language
- **Helpful** — clear guidance, low jargon, action-oriented
- **Professional** — credible for real businesses, not gimmicky AI hype

## Positioning notes

Markio is not just a page builder. It is an AI employee that understands the business (Business Brain) and produces coordinated marketing assets from that single truth source.
