# Markio Pricing

Planned pricing tiers. Features may evolve with the roadmap; this document captures the commercial framing.

## Free

Best for trying Markio on one business.

- 1 project
- Business Brain generation
- Landing page preview
- Business launch report
- localStorage / limited workspace
- Markio branding on previews

## Starter — $29 / month

For solo owners launching one brand seriously.

- Everything in Free
- Multiple regenerations
- Save / manage projects (limited)
- Landing page drafts
- Basic brand kit outputs
- Email support

## Growth — $79 / month

For growing SMEs that need a full marketing workspace.

- Everything in Starter
- More projects
- Website generator access
- Ads + social + email generations
- AI images (quota-based)
- Publish / custom domain (when available)
- Priority support

## Agency — $199 / month

For freelancers and agencies managing multiple clients.

- Everything in Growth
- Highest project limits
- Client / team collaboration (when available)
- CRM access (when available)
- Analytics across projects
- Agency-ready export / white-label options (planned)
- Priority onboarding support
